About Sian

Location

Best Location in Darjeeling

Sian Resort & Spa is one of the best resorts in Darjeeling, which is located on a hilltop below St. Pauls School. Located at a noise free zone, the spacious elite rooms & balconies of the resort has an wide view to the kanchenjunga mountain.

Restaurant

You will love it

The restaurant is multi-cuisine, the delicious taste North Indian, South Indian, Continental & Bengali dishes will encourage you to comment satisfactory that we are the best among 4 star hotels in Darjeeling. Green lawn & landscaped garden at Retreat Sian Resort & Spa, provide you to make your leisure as per your preference.

Rooms

Classic spacious rooms

With 45 well appointed rooms with balcony and clear view of Mt Kanchenjunga, the resort boasts of best facilities and largest open seating area to enjoy a mesmerising view of Darjeeling and the mountains. The only resort with fully functional discotheque. The spa inside the hotel premise is a place to relax and rejuvenate your mind and body.

Speciality of Sian

This is a luxurious place to stay with beautiful, wood floored rooms which opens to a spacious balcony with a great view and seating for two. There are beautiful sittings that have been arranged in the outside which give direct view of the grand Kanchanganga.
